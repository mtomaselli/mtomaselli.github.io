Interests in IT

My interest in I.T began when i was around 5. It was my birthday and my parents had just bought me a playstation 2 which at the time was the pinnacle of gamimg performance. My first ever console, i plugged it in and was instantly blown away with what i could do with it, how by pressing certain buttons performed specific actions. I remember thinking in my childlike mind that it was like watching a movie, except i was in direct in control of how the story played out, this was the exact moment i became obsessed with technology. Than the game boy came out and now i could take my experiences on the go, followed by the Nintendo DS which included touch features which made me feel closer with the game, than the Wii which added gestures and movements so you were now physically playing the game, i than bought an xbox 360 which was my first oonline gaming experience so not only could i play on my own but with my friends and anyone in the world. When i think about it gaming introduced me to technology that i didnt think was possible, but what i really fell in love with was the rapid advancements in technology. That is my main interest in I.T, i want to be part of the next breakthrough, the next advancements, in my short life i have seen the world go from using small, compact, button heavy phones to phones with no buttons that you can open with your face. I am a firm believer that technology is the future and if we can achieve so much progress in only 20 years, who knows what the future holds. Im fascinated how hardware and software combine to achieve a goal, or multiple goals. Just like when i was a child i dont know what the next advancement is, recently after a customer came into work and paid for his coffee with the ring on his finger which contained a chip connected to his bank, i learnt that that was now a thing and couldn't believe it, even as a mature young adult im still being blown away. I.T for me is more than just gadgets, eventually evolution will stop being biological and become technological as we begin to rely more and more on it, and i want to be right in the center of the next wave of technology. .

Personal Information

I am a 20 year old student studying a bachelor of information technology at RMIT university. I live in the northern suburbs of Melbourn with my family which includes my parents, older sister and younger brother. I come from an italian background and can speak small amounts of italian. I love to travel and am planning to return to europe in the near future. I studied at St Monicas College in Epping where i completed my VCE. I was accepted into Latrobe University and studied Law/commerce for a year but than decided it wasnt for me. I worked full time and still part time as a Bar-tender for a local restaurant. After taking some time to think about what i wanted to do i realised that i've always loved technology and have been around it ever since i was a kid and so i applied to Rmit. My main hobbies are working on my car and playing videogames with my brother as it has been a valued past time of mine since i was old enough to hold a controller.
